Customer Churn Analysis

Identifying behavioral patterns to reduce customer attrition through data-driven customer segmentation.

Problem

Customer churn reduces lifetime value and directly impacts revenue stability. The challenge was to identify behavioral signals that indicate early churn risk.

Objective

Segment customers based on engagement and purchasing behavior to support targeted retention strategies.

Methodology

1. Exploratory Data Analysis

Analyzed distributions, correlations, and missing values to understand behavioral drivers.

2. Feature Engineering

Created recency, frequency, average order value, and engagement-based indicators.

3. Customer Segmentation

Applied clustering to identify distinct churn-risk groups.

Key Insights

  • Recency outperformed frequency as churn predictor
  • Low engagement clusters showed highest churn risk
  • High-value users churned slower but required proactive monitoring

Strategic Recommendations

  • Trigger automated alerts after inactivity thresholds
  • Prioritize retention campaigns for high-LTV clusters
  • Test incentives via A/B experiments

Next Steps

  • Develop stakeholder dashboard
